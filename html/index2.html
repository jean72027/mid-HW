Description
Emperor Penguin is the tallest genus of the penguin family.
King Penguin was considered the largest penguin before the
Emperor Penguin was discovered.
Because it is taller than the king penguin, named Emperor Penguin.
Height
110–130 cm (43–51 in) tall.
Weight
22.7 to 45.4 kg (50 to 100 lb) and varies by sex.
Chicks
The female Penguin lays one egg in May or early June,
male takes charge of their egg,
balancing it on the tops of his feet, for around 65 days until hatching
