Adelie Penguin
Description
The Adelie penguin is a species of penguin
common along the entire coast of the Antarctic continent.
It is the most widely spread penguin species.
Height
46–71 cm (18–28 in) tall.
Weight
3.6 to 6.0 kg (7.9 to 13.2 lb)in height
Chicks
Adelie penguins arrive at their breeding grounds inlate October or November.
Their nests consist of stones piled together.
Their parents take turns incubating the egg; one goes to feed
and the other stays to warm the egg.
