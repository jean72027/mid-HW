Chinstrap Penguin
Description
The adult Chinstrap penguins are black with a white edge;
the inner sides of the flippers are white.
The face is white extending behind the eyes,
the chin and throat are white, while the short bill is black.
Height
68–76 cm ( 27–30 in)
Weight
3.2 to 5.3 kg ( 7.1 to 11.7 lb)in height
Chicks
On land, they build circular nests from stones, and lay two eggs,
which are incubated by both the male and the female
for shifts around 6 days each.
